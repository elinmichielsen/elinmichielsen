wild

My Bachelor's Assignment: "What Does the Word 'Wild' Mean to Us?" This project began with a question: what does the word wild mean to us, and what does it mean to me personally? For me, it immediately brings to mind the weather—especially the wind. I think of the sound it makes, the force it carries, and the essential role it plays in nature and on our planet. Throughout this semester, I explored my personal connection to the wind. I experimented with how it feels, what it evokes in me, and the associations it stirs. These drawings are the result of that exploration—visual expressions of what the wind means to me.
